#This is a Tea recipe for 2 person

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup water
- 3/2 cup skimmed milk
- 5gm ginger
- 1 Cardamom ( you can skip this)
- 2 teaspoon sugar
- 2 teaspoon dried tea leaves(TATA tea, etc.)

Directions:
1. crush the ginger and cardmom in a mortar/pestle
2. put water and milk in a pan and heat it till it starts boiling
3. add dried tea leaves and simmer for 10 min.
4. add crushed ginger and cardmom and simmer for 2-3 minutes
5. add sugar and simmer for 5 minutes
6. Your Adarak-wali-chai is Ready :)
